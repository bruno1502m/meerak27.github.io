Inspiration

We wanted to showcase the multipurpose Blacksmith cafe which is a group favorite spot on campus. It is where we come to converse, relax, study and get caffeinated. It shows the beauty of cafe shops and how even with all the noise around you can still take a moment to sit down with your cup of coffee and enjoy yourself. It is not a place where you have to conform. People that are going through all different type of stuff, still make time in their busy schedules to have a cup of coffee. To us, that's the beauty of coffee and that's the beauty o​f Blacksmith.

Our
Space

In 2015, Blacksmith Coffee Company was founded by owners who shared a love for good coffee and wanted to create a community around it. Originally based in Abu Dhabi, they started as a coffee roastery, opened a café, and now also offer coffee consultancy services. They are passionate about coffee and have a strong commitment to ethical sourcing, studying, and working with coffee. They prioritize sustainability and enjoy exploring a variety of coffee origins, experimenting with unconventional ways of offering specialty coffee to cater to different tastes and preferences.
